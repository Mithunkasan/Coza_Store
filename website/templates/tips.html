🌿 Sustainable Fashion Tips

Small changes in your wardrobe can make a big difference for the planet 🌎

♻️
Buy Second-Hand

Give clothes a second life! Thrift stores and swaps reduce textile waste and carbon emissions.

🌱
Choose Organic Fabrics

Opt for organic cotton, bamboo, or hemp — they’re biodegradable and save up to 90% more water.

👗
Quality Over Quantity

Invest in timeless, durable pieces rather than fast fashion that fades quickly.

🧵
Repair, Don’t Replace

Fix small tears, resew buttons, or upcycle your clothes into new creative looks.

🌞
Wash Smart

Use cold water, air dry, and eco-friendly detergents to lower your clothing’s environmental impact.

💚
Support Ethical Brands

Buy from companies that are transparent about fair labor and sustainability practices.

Every outfit can be an act of kindness — to the Earth and its people 🌏
